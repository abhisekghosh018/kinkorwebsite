About M/s Jagannath Foods

M/s Jagannath Foods, owned by Mr. Kinkar Ghosh, is a reputed Chanachur and Snacks manufacturing company based in Sonarpur, Kolkata (PIN 700150). Located at 34, Vivekananda Road, By Lane – 1, P.O. & P.S. Sonarpur, our company has earned the trust of customers through quality, taste, and hygiene. We are proud to be a Trade Mark Certified and ISO Certified company — a recognition of our commitment to quality standards, food safety, and customer satisfaction. Our factory produces a wide range of traditional Bengali Chanachur, mixtures, and snacks using premium ingredients and modern processing techniques, while maintaining the authentic flavor of Bengal. At M/s Jagannath Foods, we believe in blending tradition with innovation — bringing you crispy, tasty, and hygienically packed snacks for every occasion. .
